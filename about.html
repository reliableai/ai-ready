About This Course

AI-Ready Engineers is dedicated to engineers and practitioners who want to be ready to build complex systems with AI — and for AI. The course lays the foundations for building reliable systems efficiently, and for mastering the evaluation and improvement process that keeps them reliable as they grow.

Participants learn to build AI-powered applications hands-on, and develop the rigorous evaluation frameworks essential for deploying AI in the real world.
