What Happens When Eval Goes Wrong

The measurement uncertainty described here becomes organizational damage through a predictable cascade:

  • Week 2: First consequential failure from untested edge cases causes stakeholder trust cliff.
  • Week 4: Teams discover in production what should have been found in evaluation, requiring costly architectural rework.
  • Week 12: Pilot purgatory — systems stuck in post-production validation loops because evaluation frameworks never measured what production demands.

The pattern repeats: agentic AI pilots that "succeed" in curated evaluation fail within weeks of production deployment. This isn't unpredictable chaos; it's a systematic failure with recognizable timelines.

— From a conversation with Filip Deryckere
